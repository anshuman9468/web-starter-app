## 🎤 Pro Tips

1. **Switch Modes Fast**: Just say the trigger phrase
   - "Tell me a story" → Switches to Story
   - "Cook pasta" → Switches to Cooking
   - "I'm stressed" → Switches to Wellness

2. **Speak Clearly**: Clear speech = faster transcription

3. **Use Short Commands**: "Time?" works as well as "What time is it?"

4. **Enable WebGPU**: Chrome flags → WebGPU = 2x faster

5. **Quiet Environment**: Better VAD detection = faster response

---

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Slow responses?**
- Close other tabs (free RAM)
- Enable WebGPU in browser
- Use shorter commands

**Mic not working?**
- Check browser permissions (padlock icon)
- Ensure localhost or HTTPS
- Try different browser

**Models not loading?**
- Check console (F12) for errors
- Ensure 2GB+ RAM available
- Refresh page and retry
